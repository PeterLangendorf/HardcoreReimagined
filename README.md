# Red's Survival Overhaul
## Description
Red's Survival Overhaul is a mod that aims to make Minecraft harder in every way. Through a variety of features and config options, you can elevate your hardcore Minecraft experience to a new level.
## Features
### Temperature
- Temperature changes with things such as surrounding blocks, biome, time of day, elevation, armor, and more.
- When your temperature is too low or too high you will get hypothermia / heat stroke.
- Makes the nether much harder (VERY hot) and the end much harder (EXTREMELY cold).
- Adds a gauge to the hotbar that will show you your temperature.
### Environment Nerf
- Crop growth speed is fully configurable and scalable with difficulty.
- Animal breeding cooldown as well as baby growth speed is also configurable and scalable with difficulty.
- Chicken egg laying cooldown is configurable and scalable with difficulty.
- Nerfs bone meal depending on difficulty.
- Adds a passive exhaustion to the player so that you can't just AFK.
### Food Nerf
- Adds a global hunger multiplier scaling on difficulty.
- Overrides all food values to be normalized and far less.
- Adds a food history system preventing you from eating the same food more than a few times in a row.
### Health Nerf
- Adds a variety of debuffs when you're at low health or hunger.
- Prevents death resets by modifying respawn health and hunger.
- Prevents the player from dealing knockback when at low health.
### Config
- General config values are in the standard config folder in the forge config files
- There are also more specific JSON files that allow more configurability.
